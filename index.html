About me

Hi there! I'm Jason Weismueller from Essen, Germany. I currently work as an Analyst at Power Ledger in Perth, Western Australia. I'm also a doctoral researcher at The University of Western Australia. My research focuses on the negative side of social media such as the spread of misinformation and the increasingly polarised political discourse on social media.

I graduated from Brunel University in London, England with first-class honours (4.0 GPA) in Marketing and spent one semester studying Business Administration at The University of Western Australia.
