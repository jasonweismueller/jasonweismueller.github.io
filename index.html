About

Jason works as a Lecturer in Marketing at The University of Western Australia (UWA). His research focuses on the negative side of social media such as the spread of misinformation and the increasingly polarised political discourse on social media. He is also interested in many topics that are related to digital marketing. He has been teaching multiple units at UWA, including Digital Marketing, Big Data in Marketing, Introduction to Marketing, and Data Storytelling.

Please find his most recent journal and media articles in the below:
